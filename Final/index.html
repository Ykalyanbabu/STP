Towards Shillong Traffic Pulse

Real-Time Insights for Smarter Mobility.

Shillong Traffic Pulse is a dedicated platform providing real-time traffic updates across Shillong. We help commuters stay informed about road conditions, congestion, and diversions.

Our goal is to support safer, smarter, and faster travel within the city. The platform brings together live alerts, traffic advisories, and road safety information.
